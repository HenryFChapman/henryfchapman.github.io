Most of my work centered on homicides, but I assisted on financial fraud, domestic violence and other violent felonies. While in New York, I led the technical side of New York State’s first homicide charged as [domestic terrorism](https://www.washingtonpost.com/graphics/2019/local/race-war-murder-hate-crime/) and a year-long cyberstalking investigation. I did my capstone project on the mechanics of international arms trafficking across Latin American borders. While not studying, I worked in technology at Yale and in the US Intelligence Community. I’m proficient in Python, scripting, and computer forensics tools like Magnet Axiom and Cellebrite. I have testified in multiple homicide trials, depositions, and government hearings.
